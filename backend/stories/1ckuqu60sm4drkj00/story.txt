Police are investigating the fatal shooting of UnitedHealthcare CEO Brian Thompson, suspecting the killer traveled from Atlanta to New York City on a Greyhound bus. The NYPD has released photos of a person of interest in the case. Thompson was shot by a masked gunman while heading to an investor meeting at the Hilton Hotel. Shell casings at the scene had the words "deny," "defend," and "depose" inscribed on them. The suspect, captured on surveillance, fled on a bicycle into Central Park. A $10,000 reward is offered for information leading to the arrest of the suspect. UnitedHealth Group expressed gratitude for the support received and emphasized their focus on supporting Thompson's family and ensuring employee safety. The investigation is ongoing, with authorities seeking public assistance in identifying the suspect.
